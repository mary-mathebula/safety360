Case Studies

Challenge: The company needed a dependable service provider to support plant operations by managing routine maintenance, safety compliance checks, and the timely supply and movement of critical materials and equipment, all while ensuring minimal disruption to production schedules.

Our Solution: The Director of Safety360 & Services Management personally oversaw the project and coordinated a team of skilled professionals equipped with modern tools, safety systems, and industry-standard processes. We implemented structured maintenance and compliance schedules, managed the delivery and offloading of essential materials efficiently, and ensured that all safety protocols were strictly followed.

Outcome: Plant operations continued without interruptions. Anglo American Platinum benefited from streamlined processes, reducing operational downtime. The client praised our hands-on approach, professionalism, and strict adherence to safety standards, reinforcing our reputation as a reliable international service partner.

Challenge: AVI Limited required a reliable service provider to manage the delivery of office equipment across multiple sites and provide maintenance services for air-conditioning systems, ensuring minimal disruption to office operations and staff productivity.

Our Solution: The Director of Safety360 & Services Management personally oversaw the project, deploying a skilled team with experience in logistics, equipment handling, and HVAC maintenance. We coordinated timely deliveries of office equipment, implemented scheduled maintenance for air-conditioning units, and ensured all work adhered to safety and operational protocols.

Outcome: Office operations remained seamless with no disruptions. Air-conditioning systems were maintained efficiently, enhancing staff comfort and productivity. AVI Limited commended our professionalism, reliability, and ability to provide integrated solutions under one service provider.

Challenge: Valterra Platinum required a reliable cleaning and facility management service provider to maintain high hygiene standards across its processing plant, offices, and staff areas.
